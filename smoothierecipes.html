How do you make a banana smoothie from scratch?


Roughly chop banana and orange quarters then add to a blender.
Top with yogurt and the water (or milk).
Turn blender on and blend until creamy and smooth.
Taste then adjust with honey if needed.

How to make mango smoothie with a hint of lime!!!


Peel your mango to get the flesh
Pour the milk and yoghurt in your blender.
Add the chopped mango and lime juice.
Add ice cubes if you are not using frozen mango.
Blend for about 20 to 30 seconds or until smooth.
Taste then adjust with honey if needed.
